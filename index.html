Cassie Pinsent Art

Welcome to my online portfolio! My name is Cassie and I am a Norfolk based Artist. This website is the hub of my creative work, featuring amass of my photography, commissioned pieces, and paintings. I specialise in Animal portraits, but am very competent to take on any challenge! I have a first class honours degree from the University of Greenwich in Film Production, I worked as a photographer for many years in the Norfolk Broads, but also developed my skills as a hobby. Please explore my portfolio, and use the contact page in order to enquire about any commissioned work or to purchase prints. Online shop coming soon!
